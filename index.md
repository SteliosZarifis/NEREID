### 🌊 Our Approach: The Closed-Loop Ecosystem

NEREID² is the first fully autonomous, self-sustaining marine platform that turns the pollution it collects into the energy it needs to survive. We operate across four seamless layers:

#### 1. Space-Grade Intelligence
We utilize Copernicus Sentinel-2 satellite imagery to continuously map the Aegean Sea. Our systems identify the spectral signatures of microplastic patches and predict their drift, giving us a real-time map of the crisis.

#### 2. AI Coordination
A centralized AI "brain" analyzes the satellite data. Instead of deploying expensive vessels, the system automatically determines the most critical hotspots and coordinates our physical fleet for optimal interception.

#### 3. Drone Deployment
Marine-grade drones act as our rapid-response logistics. Receiving coordinates from the AI, they autonomously transport and precisely deploy our sensor-equipped buoys directly into the pollution patches. 

#### 4. The BioLantern Buoy
Once in the water, the BioLantern actively filters microplastics. Crucially, it is powered by **Microbial Fuel Cells**. Electrogenic bacteria inside the buoy consume dissolved marine pollutants—like hydrocarbons—and convert them directly into electricity. **The more polluted the water, the more power our buoys generate.**

### 🌍 Our Impact

* **Continuous Surveillance:** Moving from episodic snapshots to 24/7 autonomous monitoring.
* **Zero-Emission Logistics:** Eliminating the carbon footprint of vessel-based tracking.
* **Pollution-Powered:** Utilizing cutting-edge bioremediation to power hardware in the harshest environments.
* **Empowering Communities:** Captured plastic cartridges are retrieved by local fishing networks, supplementing coastal incomes and feeding into certified recycling supply chains.
